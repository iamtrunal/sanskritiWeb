About School of Law & Legal Studies

Best Law College in India

The School of Law at Sanskriti University offers specialized law programmes with a full coverage of law courses such as corporate law, taxation laws, Intellectual Property Rights (IPR), cyber law etc.

The objective at School of Law is to produce competent legal professionals to confidently take up legal and policy challenges of the future. Global corporations require legal guidance at every step for execution of collaborations, Mergers & Acquisitions (M&As), Joint Ventures (JVs), technology transfers, patents & intellectual property rights, labor laws etc.

B.Com. LLB (Hons)

The integrated B.Com. LLB (Hons) programme is focused on various facets of law in an economic context. This programme aims to accomplish this goal by combining the knowledge of economic sciences, including subjects like Accounts, Finance & Economics, and law.
